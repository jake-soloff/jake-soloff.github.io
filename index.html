This fall I am starting as a PhD student in statistics at the University of California, Berkeley. My interests include Bayesian nonparametric statistics, large-scale statistical inference, partial exchangeability and other probabilistic symmetries, and unsupervised acoustic modeling.

I studied mathematics as an undergraduate at Brown University. My work experience includes data science internships at the New York Times and FarmLogs, and I have experience as a teaching assistant both at Brown and the Rhode Island Deparment of Corrections.
