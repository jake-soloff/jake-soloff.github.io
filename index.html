About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Statistics at UC Berkeley, co-advised by Aditya Guntuboyina and Michael I. Jordan. Previously, I received an ScB in mathematics at Brown University.

My current research relates to shape constrained estimation in a variety of contexts, including high-dimensional statistics and nonparametric empirical Bayes.
